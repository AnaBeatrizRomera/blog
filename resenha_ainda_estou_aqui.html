Review - I'm Still Here

A look into the emotional journey of the film

I'm Still Here (2023)

Director: Fernanda Torres

Cast: Fernanda Torres, Other Actors...

Genre: Drama

Synopsis: "I'm Still Here" is a Brazilian drama that explores the complexities of aging, loneliness, and the search for meaning in life. The plot follows the protagonist on an emotional journey full of reflections about the past, present, and future.

Review: With an outstanding performance by Fernanda Torres, "I'm Still Here" stands out for its sensitivity and thematic depth. The careful direction and well-written script make this a touching and engaging film.
